New game project: Beats into Shape

A rhythm game where you forge items from the very beginning of the process.

Welcome to our submission for YH4F 2024

We create a rhythmic adventure where you craft items from start to finish.

Gameplay

You play as a smith in your forge, making objects in rhythm with the music.

The game contains 5 levels with each level representing every step to create a nice and flawless metallic object.

The Universe of Beats into Shape

In a world where music and nature intertwine, there exists a hidden valley named Harmony. At the heart of this valley lies the Ethereal Forge, a place of creation where blacksmiths work in harmony with the natural rhythms of music. Eirik, a young blacksmith, inherits this forge and discovers the fulfilling craft of blacksmithing.
